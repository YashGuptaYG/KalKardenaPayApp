Kal Kardena Pay


by


Yash Gupta


Kal Kardena Pay is a unique solution to the problem of not having money in hands and payment should be done on spot. Kal Kardena Pay is based on buy now pay later scheme in which we will be loading 10000 INR to user wallet and they can use it in shops without any hustle.

  • Business Model

    • Daily data on payments
    • Knowledge about the spending pattern
    • Regular expenses indications
    • Recommendations to convert dues to EMIs (if only facing difficulties in paying the entire amount)

  • Revenue Model

    • Income from the interests
    • Fees from the EMI partners
    • Late fees
    • Interchange income
    • Commissions earned from the partner merchants
    • It's subsidiary NBFC (Quadrillion)

  • Dependencies

    1. General Stores
    2. Rehdi Wale
    3. Banks for lending
